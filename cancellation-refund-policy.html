Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 25/05/2023

1. Cancellation of Services

1.1 Influencer Services

If you are an influencer using Incer, you may cancel your participation in campaigns or projects at any time. However, please note that cancellation after accepting a campaign may result in consequences as specified in the campaign terms agreed upon by you and the brand.

1.2 Brand Services

If you are a brand using Incer, you may cancel your campaign or project before it is accepted by an influencer. Once an influencer accepts a campaign, cancellation may not be possible, and you will be responsible for the agreed-upon compensation.

2. Refunds

2.1 Influencer Services

As an influencer, payments made to you for completed campaigns or projects are non-refundable. However, in the event of a payment error or other exceptional circumstances, please contact us at contact@incer.com, and we will review your case on an individual basis.

2.2 Brand Services

As a brand, payments made for campaigns or projects are non-refundable unless there are exceptional circumstances, such as non-delivery of services as agreed upon by the influencer. If you encounter any issues, please contact us at contact@incer.com to discuss the matter further.

Disputes

In the event of a dispute between influencers and brands, we encourage both parties to communicate and attempt to resolve the issue amicably. If a resolution cannot be reached, we may step in as a mediator to help facilitate a fair outcome. However, please note that we are not responsible for any financial disputes between influencers and brands.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our Cancellation and Refund Policy, please contact us at contact@incer.com. We will be happy to assist you and address any issues you may have.

Please note that this Cancellation and Refund Policy may be subject to change without prior notice. Any modifications or updates will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on our website or notifying you through email.

By using Incer, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Cancellation and Refund Policy.
